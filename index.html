Tamata-Chan

Information And TOS

Tamata-Chan is a Tinder-esque Discord bot. Its a simple bot where you can register your account by providing a small bio and age. After that you can start swiping and match with other registered users.

You can invite the bot using the link here.

Note: We are not responsible for the conversations created after matching or for any unverified false information people submit. Use at your own risk.

If you enjoy the bot and want to support the devs you can donate. You will be listed as a Donator on the website as well as on Discord.

Commands

The default prefix for the bot is -

help shows all the available commands.

register registers your account for matching. Provide age and bio.

update update your age or bio.

delete deletes your profile so it will no longer show for swiping.
